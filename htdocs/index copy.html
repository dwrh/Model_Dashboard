TIGGER: A Global Energy Services Model


TIGGER is an online, global computable general equilibrium (CGE) model designed to provide an empirical basis for addressing global energy challenges. TIGGER is a web-friendly version of the Trade Integrated Global Energy and Resource (TIGER) model, and is supported by the Energy Biosciences Institute at the University of California, Berkeley.


This website allows users to customize energy policy scenarios, run TIGGER with those scenarios, and view the results. Choose from the options below (click on the headings or images).


Create a Scenario and Run Tigger
The web interface allows users to customize energy policies to a future of their choosing. Through the click of a button, users can run the model and see the results.


View Model Documentation
Tigger combines a fully functional CGE model with a global economic database and a detailed representation of country- and sector-specific energy use.
