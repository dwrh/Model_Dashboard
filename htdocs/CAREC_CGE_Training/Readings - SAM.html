Workshop on a Prototype CGE Model for Mongolia

9 July 2013
National Statistical Office of Mongolia, Ulaan Baatar, Mongolia

Instructors: David Roland-Holst and Enkhbayar Shagdar

 

Background Readings

The following reference material is intended to give students further background, context, and examples of economywide modeling. These materials are referred to during the course of the training, and offer more detail on individual subjects than can be covered in lecture. All references are taken from publically available sources.

Michael Begg, Burmaa Chadraaval, Ra gchaasuren Galindev, Esmedekh Lkhanaajav and Erdenesan Eldev-Ochir(2012) “Sam for Mongolia - 2007,” GTAP 8 documentation, Purdue University.

Pyatt, Graham and JeffreyRound (1979) “Accounting and Fixed Price Multipliers in a Social Accounting Matrix Framework,” The Economic Journal, Vol. 89, No. 356. (Dec., 1979), pp. 850-873.

Roland-Holst, David, and Ferran Sancho (1995) "Modeling Prices in a SAM Structure," Review of Economics and Statistics, Vol. 77, No. 2, 361-371.

Round, Jeffery I. (1985) “Decomposing Multipliers for Economic Systems Involving Regional and World Trade,” The Economic Journal, Vol. 95, No. 378. (Jun.), pp. 383-399.
