CAPSIM

A Central Asian Policy Simulation Model


CAPSIM uses a global computable general equilibrium (CGE) model to support national economic research and regional policy dialog across CAREC, the Central Asia Regional Economic Cooperation program. CAPSIM is supported by the Asian Development Bank and is a browser-based regional version of a global CGE model developed at UC Berkeley.


Enter the CAPSIM scenario evaluation section.
The web interface allows users to analyse economic impacts of regional policy scenarios.
