CAREC Senior Officials' Meeting

Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia: 28–29 May 2009

The Senior Officials' Meeting (SOM) reviewed progress on implementation of the CAREC sector strategies and work plans, the Program's private sector development initiative , and the CAREC Institute work plan .

It also considered the development of a CAREC results framework , was briefed on progress of the Central Asia and Caucasus Disaster Risk Management Initiative , and preparations for the 8th Ministerial Conference on CAREC.

Read the Summary of Proceedings and view the interactive agenda for presentations and background papers.
