Project Details

Aktau Port Development, Master Plan, and Feasibility Study for Port of Aktau in Kazakhstan
Country:Kazakhstan
Sector:Transport
Summary:The overall objective of the project is to make the comprehensive independent evaluation of the Aktau port future prospects. Expected outputs are traffic forecasts for 2010, 2015 and 2020; a phased master plan for the port development up to 2020; a feasibility study of short- and medium-term development; a proposal of legal and institutional strengthening of the port; and a consolidated corporative plan Aktau International Maritime Trade Port.
Date Approved:0 June 2007
Status:Completed
Funding Type:Technical Assistance
Funding Amount:?1.6 million (Transport Corridor Europe-Caucasus-Asia)
