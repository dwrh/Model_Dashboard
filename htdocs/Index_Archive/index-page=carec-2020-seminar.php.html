Seminar on CAREC 2020

Baku, Azerbaijan: 7 June 2011


Seminar speakers shared their views and insights on the key elements of the proposed 10-year strategic framework for CAREC (CAREC 2020), including operational priorities, action plan of regional projects, overall institutional framework, and resource mobilization, including public-private partnership opportunities. An open forum session followed the presentations.

Participants were CAREC country delegations and representatives of the Asian Development Bank, the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development, the International Monetary Fund, the Islamic Development Bank, the United Nations Development Programme, and the World Bank.
