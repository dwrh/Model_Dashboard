Events

Regional Cooperation Bringing People Together

Every year, CAREC conducts and supports many diverse events that bring people together to share and learn about the opportunities to achieve the overarching goal of regional economic cooperation.

The Events database provides information on CAREC events such as the ministerial conferences, senior officials' meetings, and sector committee meetings.
