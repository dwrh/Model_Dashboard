CAREC Senior Officials' Meeting

Almaty, Kazakhstan: 27-28 June 2013

More than 100 participants from ministries and government agencies, national focal points, and representatives of multilateral development partners gathered for the midyear CAREC Senior Officials' Meeting.

The meeting discussed

  • key issues and progress made in implementing the CAREC Program in transport, trade facilitation, trade policy, and energy;
  • updates on the Mid-Term Review of the Transport and Trade Facilitation Strategy and Implementation Action Plan; and
  • highlights of the Annual Report on CAREC Corridor Performance Measurement and Monitoring (CPMM).

Drafts of the 2012 CAREC Development Effectiveness Review and the CAREC Institute Work Plan 2013-2017 were also presented.

The meeting was chaired by Mr. Timur Zhaxylykov, vice minister for Kazakhstan's Ministry of Economy and Budget Planning and CAREC national focal point.
