CAREC e-Alert

Keeping Updated on CAREC

CAREC e-Alert, the Program’s electronic newsletter, provides updates on recently approved projects in the region, on the latest and upcoming institutional and sector-related events, and on regional cooperation–themed and sector-specific studies and research on regional cooperation.

CAREC e-Alert / Issue No. 26 / 22 Oct-9 Nov 2012

Features
New CAREC 2020 action plan endorsed by CAREC Ministers
The Wuhan Action Plan endorsed at the 11th Ministerial Conference charts the next steps of the CAREC Program. It covers sector operational priorities, the CAREC Institute, and the transport facilitation action plan. Read more.
11th MC prioritizes the implementation plan for
CAREC 2020
Senior government officials of CAREC member countries and representatives of multilateral institution partners agreed to prioritize the implementation plan of CAREC 2020, the strategic framework endorsed at the 10th Ministerial Conference (MC). Visit the 11th MC page.
CAREC national focal points meet a day before the
11th Ministerial Conference
CAREC national focal points met a day before the 11th Ministerial Conference to receive updates on priority sectors, focus on CAREC 2020 implementation, discuss CAREC Institute's future direction, and be updated in progress made in the corridor development study. Visit the CAREC Senior Officials' Meeting page.
CAREC trade policy focal points meet in Wuhan
CAREC trade policy sector focal points met in Wuhan, People's Republic of China, to focus on topics such as borderless bazaars in Central Asia, the Customs Union and Kazakhstan's imports, the World Trade Organization training workshop, and the committee's future work program. Visit the 17th TPCC Meeting page.
CAREC customs officials attend 11th annual meeting
CAREC customs officials attended the 11th Customs Cooperation Committee annual meeting in Chengdu, People's Republic of China, to review progress in priority areas, discuss how to strengthen links, and agree on a proposed list of trade facilitation projects. Visit the 11th CCC Meeting page.

The CAREC Program website--www.carecprogram.org; www.carecprogram.org/ru-- serves as a comprehensive hub focusing on the full spectrum of CAREC Program work and activities in Central Asia and neighboring subregions.

Supporting the work of the Program in the transport, energy, and trade sectors, the site offers, in Russian and English:

  • full information and documentation relating to the CAREC Program
  • a searchable database of CAREC-related projects with links to original documents
  • a searchable studies and training database with links to original documents
  • a searchable events database providing links to papers and presentations from each event
  • country pages for each CAREC country with links to CAREC projects, contacts, country-specific features, national strategies, and other key documents in the CAREC priority sectors

CAREC is a partnership of 10 countries working together - Afghanistan, Azerbaijan, the People's Republic of China, Kazakhstan, the Kyrgyz Republic, Mongolia, Pakistan, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, and Uzbekistan – and supported by 6 multilateral institutions - Asian Development Bank, European Bank for Reconstruction and Development, International Monetary Fund, Islamic Development Bank, United Nations Development Programme, and World Bank - to create economic growth and improve the quality of life for all people of the CAREC region.

Your feedback on the site and contributions are most welcome; email info@carecprogram.org
