Project Details
Rehabilitation of Bamian-Yakawlang Road
Country:Afghanistan
Sector:Transport
Summary:The project component of the North–South Corridor Project involves
  1. rehabilitation of the 98.9-kilometer Bamian–Yakawlang road to an asphalt-paved standard that will allow smooth passage of heavy vehicles with two traffic lanes.
Expected Outcome:The road project is part of the North–South Corridor project which will help promote economic and social development and reduce poverty in Afghanistan.
Expected Outputs:
  1. Rehabilitated Bamian–Yakawlang road.
Date Approved:26 September 2006
Status:Completed
Expected Completion:25 June 2010
Funding Type:Grant
Funding Amount:$36.3 million (Grant - Asian Development Fund) (Asian Development Bank)
$20.0 million (Grant - Japan Fund for Poverty Reduction) (Asian Development Bank)
$800,000 (Government of Afghanistan)
Links/Documents:ADB Project Page
