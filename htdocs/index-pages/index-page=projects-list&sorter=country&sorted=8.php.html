All Key Projects
From 2000 to 2011, more than 300 investment projects in transport, trade, and energy have been financed and implemented by the CAREC countries and development partners: with growing interest this number is set to increase significantly over the next 10 years.

Presenting All Projects

This database tracks all key projects in transport, trade, and energy along the CAREC corridors and throughout the region.

Each project entry includes summary details such as country coverage, priority area or sector, approval dates, funding type and amount, and related links and documents. CAREC projects, highlight in yellow, provide additional information.
