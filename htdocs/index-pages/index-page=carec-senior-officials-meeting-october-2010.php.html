CAREC Senior Officials' Meeting

Cebu, Philippines: 31 October 2010


The CAREC senior officials met prior to the 9th Ministerial Conference to review progress and make recommendations to the Ministers. The senior officials considered the following:

  • commemorative activities for the 10th anniversary of CAREC's institutional framework in 2011;
  • the CAREC Institute Performance Assessment Review;
  • progress and issues in the priority sectors;
  • Pakistan's membership application; and
  • Senior Officials' Report to the Ministers.

The officials agreed that this year’s Development Effectiveness Review is presented as an "Introduction" in the form of a manual and presents the first completed results framework; it provides baseline values for several indicators against which future progress will be measured.

See the Report of the Senior Officials' Meeting to the CAREC Ministers for more details.
